This webpage is about quantum computers and how they work, there uses, and what the past and future of quantum computing will look like.

Quantum computing is an emergent field of cutting-edge computer science harnessing the unique qualities of quantum mechanics to solve problems beyond the ability of even the most powerful classical computers, making them some of the best computers in the whole world.
